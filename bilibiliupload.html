  • title (str) – video’s title
  • tid (int) – video type, see: https://member.bilibili.com/x/web/archive/pre or https://github.com/uupers/BiliSpider/wiki/%E8%A7%86%E9%A2%91%E5%88%86%E5%8C%BA%E5%AF%B9%E5%BA%94%E8%A1%A8
